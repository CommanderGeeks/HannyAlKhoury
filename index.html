The Journey

Born in 1990 in Eilabun, the only Palestinian village whose people were allowed to return after the Nakba of 1948, Hanny Al Khoury's art is inseparable from his identity as a Palestinian. His journey from homelessness in Jerusalem to international recognition is a testament to the power of resilience and artistic vision.

After immigrating to Canada in 2017, Al Khoury found the space to process his trauma and transform it into powerful visual narratives. His work has been exhibited at Banksy's Walled Off Hotel, the Art Gallery of Alberta, and galleries across Abu Dhabi, New York, and Paris.

Each painting is a station in his life's journey—capturing broken emotions, misery, and trauma, while simultaneously celebrating resistance, hope, and the indomitable human spirit. His 2023 museum exhibition "La La Lands" at the Art Gallery of Alberta marked his arrival as a major voice in contemporary Palestinian art.
